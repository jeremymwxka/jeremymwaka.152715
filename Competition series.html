BMW M5 Competition Series

The BMW M5 Competition Series is an enhanced version of the standard M5, designed for enthusiasts seeking even greater performance and exclusivity. Some notable features of the Competition Series include:

  • Increase in Power: The Competition Series boasts an upgraded engine output of 617 horsepower, delivering exhilarating acceleration and speed.
  • Enhanced Suspension: Specially-tuned suspension components provide sharper handling and improved agility, enhancing the car's dynamic capabilities on both road and track.
  • Distinctive Design Elements: Unique exterior accents, such as blacked-out trim and exclusive badging, distinguish the Competition Series from the standard M5 model.
  • Exclusive Interior Touches: The interior of the Competition Series features special upholstery options, carbon fiber trim, and other bespoke details, elevating the cabin ambiance.
  • Performance Package: Optional performance upgrades, such as carbon-ceramic brakes and lightweight wheels, further enhance the car's track performance and driving experience.

The BMW M5 Competition Series represents the pinnacle of performance and luxury within the M5 lineup, offering discerning drivers an unparalleled driving experience.
