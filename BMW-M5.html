BMW M5 Specs

The BMW M5 is a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional performance and luxurious features. Here are some detailed specifications:

  • Engine: 4.4-liter TwinPower Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 600 hp @ 6,000 - 6,500 RPM
  • Torque: 553 lb-ft @ 1,800 - 5,860 RPM
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 3.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (electronically limited, optional increase to 190 mph with M Driver's Package)
  • Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission with Drivelogic
  • Drive Type: Rear-wheel drive (xDrive all-wheel drive available)
  • Curb Weight: Approximately 4,370 lbs
  • Fuel Economy: Combined city/highway MPG varies, typically around 17 MPG
  • Braking: High-performance M compound brakes with optional carbon-ceramic brakes
